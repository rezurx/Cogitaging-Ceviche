Your Résumé Is Dying. The Blockchain Is Replacing It.

Conrad T. Hannon

July 10, 2025

Image created with generative AI In the near future, credentials won’t be uploaded. They’ll be minted, staked, and scanned. For decades, résumés have been the primary mechanism for establishing trust in the professional world. A tidy document, filled with bullet points, links, and degrees, meant to signal competence and readiness. But we all know the truth: résumés are broken. They’re easy to fake. They’re hard to verify. They don’t capture your actual skills or contributions. Just the things you say you’ve done. In a world moving faster, more remote, and more decentralized, the résumé is becoming more of a liability…
